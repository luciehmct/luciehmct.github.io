    48C: A Live Biotherapeutic Product to Catch Cadmium

    iGEM - EPFL

    Context: Cadmium is a toxic heavy metal and carcinogen that contaminates soil and water, bioaccumulating in crops and posing a serious risk for chronic human exposure. Our iGEM team engineered a live biotherapeutic product (LBP): a modified *E. coli* designed to "catch" cadmium ions in the intestine. The engineered bacteria express a mucus-binding protein (`SpaC`) to anchor in the gut and a cadmium-specific binding protein (`EC20`) to sequester the metal, which is then safely cleared from the body.
